About

Grita was born from a simple idea: karaoke should feel like an escape. Not a tourist trap. Not a cramped afterthought. A destination.

Tucked into the heart of Barcelona's Gràcia neighborhood, we've created intimate private rooms where you can sing your heart out—or watch your friends embarrass themselves—while sipping proper cocktails and Japanese whisky.

Every room features a curated library of over 50,000 songs spanning decades and languages. Premium sound. Moody lighting. And a bar menu designed to fuel the best nights you'll barely remember.
